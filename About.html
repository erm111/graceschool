HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

Dominion Academy Group of Schools, Ikot Ekong, Mkpat Enin Local Government Area, Akwa Ibom State was founded on Monday 20th September, 2004 with just three pupils. The opening ceremony was brief and solemn but bold and highly inspiring.Amidst a little group of witnesses, the name was declared "Dominion Academy". The vision bearer and amiable Director, Elder Joe Nnah Ikpe pronounced the motto "Our God, our Help". ....the dream keeps unfolding day after day.
